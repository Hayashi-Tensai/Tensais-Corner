Enchanter

Gameplay
Enchanter is a 2D action typing game where you will face two different bosses Ifrit and Qareen, Each of the boss will have their own unique skills.There 3 skills you can use to fight the bosses, fireball, feal and stun. Type your way to glory in this short but sweet typing adventure.

Background Story
Once upon a time, There is a jinni that is sick of granting wishes, the jinni then decided to summon demons to the land and destroy the humans. You play as a battle mage on a quest to exorcise these demons and defeat the jinni once and for all.
